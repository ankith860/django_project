ABOUT THE BLOG

Coding has always peaked my curiosity, and I recently set out to learn a thing or two about it. This blog is essentially my first big software project, and it taught me a lot! The blog is mostly a testing ground for me to learn software skills and networking concepts. So if you are a user, well I did say this is my FIRST big project. Stick around!

ABOUT ME

I am a University of Maryland, College Park alum with a Bachelor of Science in Physics, you can see more about my technical experience and qualifications in my resume in the section below. If you are interested in who I am as a person, a few things come to mind.
